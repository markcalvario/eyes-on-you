What are your rights?

A good resource to use is a website called: Immigration Defense Project .
FACTS:

  • ICE often pretends to be police, here's what you can do: Ask them to slip their card under the door or a warrant.
  • You don't have to let them in without a warrent SIGNED by the judge.
  • DON'T LIE.
  • DON'T RUN OR PHYSICALLY RESIST ARREST.
  • You have the right to not talk to them.
